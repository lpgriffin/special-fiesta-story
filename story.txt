Once upon a time, there was a young man named Hans. Hans lived in a small secluded shack deep inside a forest. He enjoyed a simple, contented life, isolated from the stresses of ordinary life.
Sometimes, however, Hans did wonder what it was like to be around other people. One day, his curiosity burgeoned to the point where he felt compelled to do something about it...
Hans decided to pack his things and head north to find out what was in the wide world
